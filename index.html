FIFA World Cup

(Team Performance Review)

This graph shows values of two variables plotted along two axes (X-axis as year, Y-axis as number of goals).
The pattern of the resulting points reveals country that make number of goals in that FIFA world cup year.
Each point color represents different country. On highlighting the point shows country name, year and number of goals below the scatter plot.

Based on selected year, below bar chart shows number of goals by participated country in the FIFA world cup of the selected year.
On highligting the bar will show the details of country and number of goals as a tool tip.

This chart provides the information of overall perforamnce of the teams in the worldcup based on number of goals
The team which performed more goals has been with larger area and teams with less number of goals made during the world cup with lesser area. Here it was considered overall goals made during all the world cups.
